Undergraduate Program

MCS offers undergraduate degrees in two streams namely Electrical (Telecommunication) Engineering and Software Engineering.[1] Each year a standard NET (NUST Entrance Test) is conducted for the undergraduate enrollment in these courses.

The students are also enrolled via SAT subject tests. Some students are also selected as Gentlemen Cadets of Pakistan army through ISSB. Moreover, wards of army personnel are also selected as PC's(Paying Cadets) and ASC's (Additional Selected Cadets).
